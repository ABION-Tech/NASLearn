Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NAS

Is NAS really completely free?

Yes! NAS is 100% free - no hidden costs or fees. ABION Industries covers all tuition, materials, and resources. We even provide data support for participants who need it during the program.

What are the eligibility requirements?

We're looking for ambitious Africans aged 18-35 who are either recent graduates, currently unemployed, or underemployed. You need consistent internet access, basic computer skills, and 15-20 hours per week to commit to the program. Most importantly, we seek candidates with drive and commitment to transform their careers.

How does the internship work?

After 3 months of training, you'll spend 3 months interning with ABION Industries on real projects. This is a paid internship where you'll apply your new skills under professional guidance. Many interns receive job offers from ABION or our partner companies upon completion.

Do I need prior experience in my chosen field?

No prior experience is required for most programs. Our proprietary NAS Technique is designed to take students from beginner to job-ready professional in 6 months. For advanced programs like Machine Learning, we recommend some foundational knowledge in mathematics or programming.

What happens after I graduate?

Graduates receive NAS certification and join our alumni network. We provide 6 months of career support including job placement assistance, reference letters, and networking opportunities. Many graduates receive job offers through our corporate partners or during their ABION internship.
