Practice Exercises

1. Campaign Creation Exercise:

Create a mock PPC campaign for a fictional product (e.g., eco-friendly water bottles). Outline the target audience, proposed keywords, ad copy (headlines and descriptions), and selected ad extensions.

Solution Outline:
  • Target Audience: Eco-conscious consumers aged 18-45.
  • Proposed Keywords: "eco-friendly water bottles," "reusable water bottles," "sustainable drinkware."
  • Ad Copy:
    • Headline 1: "Stay Hydrated Sustainably!"
    • Headline 2: "Shop Eco-Friendly Water Bottles."
    • Description: "Discover our range of stylish and sustainable water bottles. Free shipping on orders over $50!"
  • Ad Extensions:
    • Sitelink: "View Our Collection"
    • Callout: "Free Shipping Available"
2. Metrics Analysis Exercise:

Analyze a provided set of PPC campaign metrics (CTR, CPC, conversions) and determine the effectiveness of the campaign.

Solution Example:
  • Metrics Provided:
    • CTR: 3%
    • CPC: $0.75
    • Conversions: 50
    • Total Spend: $750
  • Analysis:
    • Calculate the total clicks: Total Spend / CPC = $750 / $0.75 = 1000 clicks.
    • Calculate the conversion rate: Conversions / Total Clicks = 50 / 1000 = 5%.
    • Discuss potential optimizations based on metrics.
