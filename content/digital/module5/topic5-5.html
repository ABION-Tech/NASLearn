Practice Exercises

1. Campaign Design Exercise:

Task: Create a social media campaign for a fictional eco-friendly startup focusing on sustainable products.

Solution Outline:

Campaign Goals: Increase brand awareness and drive website traffic.
Target Audience: Eco-conscious millennials aged 25-35.
Content Plan:
  • Week 1: Instagram posts showcasing product usage and benefits.
  • Week 2: Facebook live session on sustainable living tips.
  • Week 3: Twitter polls to engage followers on environmental issues.
  • Week 4: LinkedIn articles discussing industry trends in sustainability.

2. Scheduling Tools Exercise:

Task: Use Hootsuite or Buffer to schedule three posts for a week for the fictional brand created in the campaign design exercise.

Solution Outline:

  • Post 1: Instagram image of the product with a caption highlighting its benefits (scheduled for Monday at 10 AM).
  • Post 2: Facebook link to a blog post about sustainable practices (scheduled for Wednesday at 2 PM).
  • Post 3: Twitter thread on environmental tips (scheduled for Friday at 12 PM).
