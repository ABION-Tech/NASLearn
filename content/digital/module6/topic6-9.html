Common Misconceptions

Email Marketing is Dead

Many believe that with the rise of social media, email marketing is obsolete. In reality, email remains one of the most effective marketing channels, boasting a high ROI.

More Subscribers Equals More Success

Quality trumps quantity. A smaller, engaged list can be more valuable than a large list of uninterested recipients.

All Emails Should Be Promotional

Providing value through informative and engaging content can foster better relationships with subscribers than solely sending promotional emails.
