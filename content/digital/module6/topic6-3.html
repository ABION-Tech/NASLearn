Measuring Email Campaign Performance

Key Metrics:

  • Open Rates: The percentage of recipients who open an email. High open rates indicate effective subject lines and a well-targeted audience.
  • Click-Through Rates (CTR): The percentage of recipients who click on a link within the email. It measures engagement and the effectiveness of the content.
  • Conversion Rates: The percentage of recipients who complete a desired action (e.g., purchase, sign-up) after clicking through.
