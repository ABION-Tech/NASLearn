Practice Exercises

1. Capstone Project Proposal:

  • Scenario: Choose a brand and outline a preliminary digital marketing strategy.
  • Task:
    • Conduct market research to identify target demographics.
    • Define marketing objectives and proposed strategies for different channels.
    • Create a timeline for the campaign.
  • Solution Outline: Provide a one-page summary detailing the chosen brand, objectives, strategies, and timeline.

2. Emerging Trends Analysis:

  • Scenario: Analyze how a specific emerging trend (e.g., AI or voice search) could impact a chosen industry (e.g., retail, healthcare).
  • Task:
    • Research current applications of the trend in the industry.
    • Propose ways businesses can adapt to leverage the trend.
  • Solution Outline: Present findings in a structured report with key insights and recommendations.
