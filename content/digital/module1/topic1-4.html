Practice Exercises in Digital Marketing

Engage with the following exercises to apply your understanding of digital marketing concepts:

1. Scenario Analysis

Imagine you are the marketing manager for a small bakery. Identify which digital marketing channels would be most effective for promoting your bakery and explain why.

Solution:
  • Social Media (Instagram and Facebook): To showcase visually appealing products.
  • Email Marketing: For sharing special offers and events.
  • SEO: To ensure the bakery appears in local searches for baked goods.

2. Keyword Brainstorming

Create a list of potential keywords that a fitness studio might use for SEO. Discuss why these keywords would be effective.

Solution:
  • Keywords: "local fitness classes," "personal training," "group workouts," "fitness tips."
  • Explanation: These keywords target local searchers and potential customers looking for specific fitness services.
