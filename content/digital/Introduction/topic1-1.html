About This Course

Welcome to the world of Digital marketing which encompasses all marketing efforts that use the internet or an electronic device. It includes a variety of tactics and channels to connect with potential customers where they spend much of their time: online. Digital marketing is at the core of most products & services earning it the nickname the new adverstisment, hence its rise in demand.

This course is designed to equip you with the essential Digital Marketing skills needed by many tech companies. Over the next several weeks, you will engage with expert-led lectures, hands-on projects, and collaborative learning activities.

Our unique learning model ensures you balance theoretical knowledge with practical application during the class phase and in your internship.

Learning Model

  • Self-paced lessons available on the LMS
  • Weekly group discussion sessions
  • Hands-on projects and peer collaboration
  • Internship opportunities for real-world experience

Total Duration: 6 months

The entire program lasts 6 months and combines coursework, electives, and an internship for a comprehensive learning experience to equip you for the future.

Core Program

10 weeks of intensive coursework

We will provide you with practical digital skills through real-world case studies and lessons that directly apply to industry needs.

Electives

2 additional weeks

During these two weeks, you'll have the opportunity to select one elective per week, choosing from exciting from subjects like AI, Entrepreneurship, Digital Marketing, and more to broaden your skills.

Internship

3-month internal internship at ABION Industries

Gain hands-on experience working on real projects within ABION Industries, preparing you for your future career or entrepreneurial goals.

Key Channels

Week 1: Introduction to Digital Marketing

Understand the basics of digital marketing, explore the importance of digital marketing in today's business environment.

Week 2: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Get familiar with the basics of SEO and how iit enhances visibility on search engines to attract organic traffic.

Week 3: Pay-Per-Click (PPC)

Understand PPC advertising and how to create effective campaigns (Paid advertisements that appear on search engine results pages).

Week 4: Content Marketing Strategy

Learn how to create and implement a content marketing strategy.

Week 5: Social Media Marketing

Engaging with users on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

Week 6: Email Marketing

Direct communication with customers through email to build relationships and drive sales.

Week 7: Analytics and Data Interpretation

Learn to analyze and interpret data to improve marketing strategies..

Week 8: Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

Understand the principles of CRO and its impact on digital marketing.

Week 9: Influencer Marketing and Affiliate Marketing

Explore the roles of influencer and affiliate marketing in digital strategies.

Week 10: Capstone Project Execution

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 11 - 12: Elective Classes

Student shall embark on a two-week journey on any 2 electives availble on our catalogue to broaden their horizons.

Week 13: Internship Preparation

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 14-23: Internship Proper

Student shall focus on internship aiming to gain industry experience and professional development.

Week 24: Final Completion and Graduation

Student shall finalize all thier works and recieve a certificate on Graduation day, for their hard work in the class session and their dedication during the internship.
