Practice Exercises

1. Mock Campaign Exercise:

Task: Create a mock email marketing campaign for a fictional fitness brand promoting a new online course.
Solution Outline:
  • Subject Line: "Transform Your Fitness Journey Today!"
  • Content:
    • Brief introduction about the course.
    • Highlight benefits (e.g., personalized plans, expert guidance).
    • Include a lead magnet (e.g., free introductory video).
    • Strong CTA: "Enroll Now for Exclusive Access!"

2. Metrics Analysis Exercise:

Scenario: Given a set of hypothetical email performance metrics (e.g., 30% open rate, 5% CTR, 1% conversion rate), analyze what these figures suggest about the campaign.
Solution Outline:
  • Open Rate Analysis: Evaluate the subject line effectiveness.
  • CTR Analysis: Assess the content relevance and CTA effectiveness.
  • Conversion Rate Analysis: Discuss potential barriers to conversion (e.g., landing page issues).
