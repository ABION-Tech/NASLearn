Content Distribution Channels

Social Media:
Definition: Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter where content can be shared and engaged with.
Significance: Social media amplifies reach and facilitates direct interaction with audiences.
SEO (Search Engine Optimization):
Definition: The practice of optimizing content to rank higher in search engine results.
Significance: SEO helps increase organic visibility, driving more traffic to the content.
Email:
Definition: A direct communication channel that allows marketers to send targeted messages to subscribers.
Significance: Email marketing has a high ROI and is effective for nurturing leads and building relationships.
