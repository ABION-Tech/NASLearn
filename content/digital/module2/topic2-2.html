On-page vs. Off-page SEO

On-page SEO:

On-page SEO refers to the techniques used directly within the website to improve its position in search rankings.

Key Elements:

  • Content Quality: High-quality, relevant, and valuable content that meets user intent.
  • Meta Tags: Title tags and meta descriptions that include keywords and summarize page content.
  • Internal Linking: Linking to other pages within the site to enhance navigation and distribute page authority.

Off-page SEO:

Off-page SEO involves actions taken outside of your own website to impact rankings.

Key Elements:

  • Backlinks: Links from other reputable websites that direct traffic to your site, signaling trustworthiness and authority to search engines.
  • Social Signals: Engagement on social media platforms that can influence visibility and reputation.
