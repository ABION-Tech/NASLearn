Digital Marketing: Week 2: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Discussion Questions

nteractive Insights on SEO Concepts

1. Impact of Keyword Research

How does effective keyword research influence a website's SEO performance?

Discussion Points:

  • How keywords connect user intent to website content.
  • The role of long-tail keywords in attracting targeted traffic.
  • The impact of keyword difficulty and competition on strategy.
  • Examples of successful campaigns driven by well-researched keywords.

2. On-Page vs. Off-Page SEO

Which SEO strategy do you think is more impactful and why?

Discussion Points:

  • On-Page SEO: Direct control over content quality, keyword optimization, and technical elements.
  • Off-Page SEO: Building domain authority through backlinks, partnerships, and social proof.
  • When each approach is most effective (e.g., new vs. established websites).

3. Technical SEO Challenges

What are common technical SEO issues, and how can they affect website rankings?

Discussion Points:

  • Examples: Slow site speed, poor mobile responsiveness, broken links, missing alt text.
  • How these issues affect user experience and search engine crawling.
  • Tools like Google Search Console and Screaming Frog for identifying and resolving these problems.

4. Trends in SEO

How might voice search and AI-driven tools change keyword optimization strategies?

Discussion Points:

  • The rise of conversational and question-based search queries due to voice assistants.
  • Shifting focus from exact-match keywords to semantic and intent-driven optimization.
  • AI tools (like ChatGPT or Google's BERT) redefining how search engines understand and rank content.
  • Preparing for future trends, such as optimizing for local voice searches or creating AI-friendly content structures.

These questions encourage critical thinking and practical application, fostering a deeper understanding of SEO strategies and challenges.
