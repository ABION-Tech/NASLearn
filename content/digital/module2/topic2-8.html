Common Misconceptions

1. SEO is a One-Time Task:

SEO is an ongoing process. Regular updates, content creation, and algorithm changes require continuous optimization efforts.

2. Keyword Stuffing Improves Rankings:

Overloading content with keywords can lead to penalties from search engines. Quality content that meets user intent is more effective.

3. Backlinks are the Only Factor for SEO Success:

While backlinks are important, SEO success is determined by various factors, including content quality, user experience, and technical optimization.
