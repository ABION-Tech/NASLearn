Practice Exercises

1. Keyword Research Exercise:

Choose a topic relevant to your niche and conduct keyword research using Google Keyword Planner. Identify at least five short-tail and five long-tail keywords.

Solution:

  • Topic: Vegan Recipes
  • Short-tail keywords: Vegan, Vegan recipes, Healthy food
  • Long-tail keywords: Easy vegan dinner recipes, Quick vegan snacks, Vegan meal prep ideas.

2. SEO Audit:

Perform an SEO audit on a website of your choice (personal blog, small business site, etc.). Identify at least three areas for improvement in terms of on-page SEO, off-page SEO, and technical SEO.

Solution:

  • On-page: Improve meta descriptions to include target keywords.
  • Off-page: Increase backlinks through guest blogging and collaborations.
  • Technical: Create and submit an XML sitemap to Google Search Console.
