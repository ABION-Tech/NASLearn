Digital Marketing: Week 2: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Assignment: Keyword Research and Blog Post Optimization

Page 5: Detailed Assignment Guide

Objective

Use keyword research and SEO techniques to optimize a blog post for better search engine visibility.

Steps

1. Select a Topic

Choose a topic based on your interests or relevant niches.

Examples:

  • "Best Coffee Shops in New York."
  • "Fitness Tips for Beginners."
  • "Affordable Travel Destinations in Europe."

2. Keyword Research

Use tools like Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, or Ubersuggest to identify:

  • Primary Keyword: A high-volume, relevant keyword central to your topic.
    • Example: Best coffee shops in New York.
  • Secondary Keywords: Related terms with moderate search volume.
    • Examples: coffee near me, artisan coffee in NYC.

Factors to consider:

  • Search volume.
  • Competition level (choose low to medium for better ranking chances).
  • Relevance to your topic and audience intent.

3. Write the Blog Post

Structure (300 words):

  • Title: Include the primary keyword (50–60 characters).
    • Example: "Discover the Best Coffee Shops in New York for Every Mood."
  • Introduction: Hook readers and introduce the topic with the primary keyword.
  • Body: Use secondary keywords naturally while providing value.
    • Highlight key features, tips, or recommendations.
  • Conclusion: Summarize and include a call-to-action (e.g., “Visit these spots today!”).

Meta Description (under 160 characters):

  • Example: "Explore the top coffee shops in New York, from hidden gems to trendy cafes. Find your perfect brew today!"

4. Submit

Include:

  • Blog Post: Clearly structured and SEO-optimized with natural keyword usage.
  • Keyword Research Details: Keywords identified and reasons for their selection.
  • Explanation: A 100-word rationale explaining how your research influenced the blog structure and focus.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Keyword Selection: Appropriateness and strategic relevance.
  • SEO Optimization: Effective keyword integration in title, headings, and meta description.
  • Readability: Well-written, engaging, and free of keyword stuffing.
  • Clarity of Explanation: Insightful reasoning for keyword choices and blog structure.

This assignment will hone students’ skills in combining technical SEO practices with creative content writing, providing a practical approach to digital marketing.
