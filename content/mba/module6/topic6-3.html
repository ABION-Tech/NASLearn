Case Studies of Successful Strategies

Explanation:

Case studies of successful strategies allow students to analyze real-world strategic decisions, learn from successes (and failures), and apply those insights to their own strategic planning.

Examples:

  • Netflix’s Transformation: Initially a DVD rental service, Netflix successfully shifted to a streaming platform, later expanding into original content production. This strategic pivot capitalized on shifting consumer behavior and positioned Netflix as a market leader.
  • Nike’s Global Expansion Strategy: By investing in celebrity endorsements, aggressive marketing, and innovative product development, Nike expanded globally and became one of the most recognizable brands in sports apparel.
