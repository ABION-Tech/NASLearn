Discussion Questions

1. Key Characteristics of Successful Entrepreneurs

What are the key characteristics that differentiate successful entrepreneurs from others? Can these traits be learned, or are they innate?

2. Fostering a Culture of Innovation

How can businesses foster a culture of innovation, and what challenges do they face when trying to implement radical or disruptive innovations?

3. Importance of Pivoting Business Models

In a rapidly changing market, how important is it for startups to pivot their business models? Can you think of examples where a pivot led to success or failure?

4. Funding Sources: Benefits and Drawbacks

What are the benefits and drawbacks of various funding sources (e.g., bootstrapping vs. venture capital)?

5. Ethical Considerations in Venture Capital

What ethical considerations should entrepreneurs take into account when seeking venture capital funding?
