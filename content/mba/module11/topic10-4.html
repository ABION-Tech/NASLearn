Exams

Format

A final exam assessing knowledge and understanding of concepts related to the capstone project, including problem-solving, business strategy, and research methodologies.

Sample Exam Questions

  1. Multiple Choice: What is the primary purpose of a feasibility study?
    • a) To analyze competition
    • b) To assess the viability of a proposed solution
    • c) To identify customer preferences
    • d) To create a marketing plan
  2. Short Answer: Describe the importance of SWOT analysis in developing business strategies. Provide an example of how it can influence decision-making.
  3. Essay Question: Discuss the role of teamwork in successfully completing a capstone project. How can team dynamics impact the outcome of the project?

Weighting

20% of overall course grade.
