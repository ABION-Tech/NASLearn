Assignments

Types of Assignments

  • Weekly Reflection Papers: Students will submit short reflection papers summarizing their learning and experiences in the capstone project process.
  • SWOT and PESTLE Analysis Assignment: Each student or group will complete a detailed analysis relevant to their project.
  • Research Proposal: A document outlining the business problem, objectives, and proposed methodology for the capstone project.

Assessment Criteria

  • Clarity of ideas and analysis.
  • Depth of research and use of relevant sources.
  • Application of theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios.

Weighting

30% of overall course grade.
