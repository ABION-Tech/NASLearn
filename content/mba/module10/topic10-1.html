Capstone Project

Assessment Criteria

  • Originality: Unique insights and innovative solutions.
  • Research Depth: Use of diverse and relevant sources to inform the project.
  • Feasibility of Solutions: Practicality and implementability of the proposed strategies.
  • Quality of Presentation: Clarity, professionalism, and engagement during the final presentation.
  • Comprehensive Report: Structure, clarity, and thoroughness of the written report.

Evaluation Process

A panel of faculty and industry experts will assess the presentations using a rubric that includes the above criteria.

Peer evaluations may also contribute to the final assessment.

Weighting

30% of overall course grade.
