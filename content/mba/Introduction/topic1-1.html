About This Course

Welcome to the MBA Essentials Course at the New Age Skillsphere (NAS). This course is designed to provide you with a solid foundation in key business management principles, preparing you to navigate the complexities of the corporate world. Whether you're considering pursuing an MBA or looking to enhance your business acumen, this course will equip you with the essential tools to succeed in leadership roles and strategic decision-making.

Throughout the course, you will engage with expert-led lectures, hands-on projects, case studies, and collaborative activities. You will cover core business topics, including strategic management, financial analysis, marketing, organizational behavior, and business ethics. You'll gain a practical understanding of how to manage teams, drive business growth, and make data-driven decisions that align with organizational goals.

Our unique learning model ensures that you not only grasp theoretical concepts but also develop the practical skills required for real-world business challenges. You will learn how to think critically, lead with confidence, and communicate effectively in diverse business environments. The course also includes an internship, where you'll apply your knowledge in a professional setting, gaining valuable experience to help you thrive in the competitive world of business management.

Learning Model

  • Self-paced lessons available on the LMS
  • Weekly group discussion sessions
  • Hands-on projects and peer collaboration
  • Internship opportunities for real-world experience

Total Duration: 6 months

The entire program lasts 6 months and combines coursework, electives, and an internship for a comprehensive learning experience to equip you for the future.

Core Program

10 weeks of intensive coursework

We will provide you with practical digital skills through real-world case studies and lessons that directly apply to industry needs.

Electives

2 additional weeks

During these two weeks, you'll have the opportunity to select one elective per week, choosing from exciting from subjects like AI, Entrepreneurship, Digital Marketing, and more to broaden your skills.

Internship

3-month internal internship at ABION Industries

Gain hands-on experience working on real projects within ABION Industries, preparing you for your future career or entrepreneurial goals.

MBA Essentials Curriculum

Module 1: Business Strategy

Learn the fundamentals of business strategy, including competitive analysis, value propositions, and market positioning.

Module 2: Financial Accounting

Understand financial statements, balance sheets, income statements, and how to analyze financial health in a business context.

Module 3: Marketing Management

Study the principles of marketing, including consumer behavior, market research, segmentation, and brand management strategies.

Module 4: Operations Management

Explore the essential aspects of operations, including supply chain management, process optimization, and production strategies.

Module 5: Organizational Behavior

Examine how organizational culture, leadership styles, and employee motivation influence business performance and decision-making.

Module 6: Business Ethics and Corporate Governance

Learn the importance of ethical business practices and governance frameworks, including decision-making and corporate social responsibility.

Module 7: Managerial Economics

Explore key economic principles, including supply and demand, pricing strategies, and the role of economics in managerial decision-making.

Module 8: Financial Management

Master financial management concepts, including capital budgeting, risk analysis, financial planning, and managing investments.

Module 9: Leadership and Change Management

Understand the principles of leadership, managing organizational change, and leading teams in a dynamic business environment.

Week 10: Capstone Project Execution

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 11 - 12: Elective Classes

Student shall embark on a two-week journey on any 2 electives availble on our catalogue to broaden their horizons.

Week 13: Internship Preparation

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 14-23: Internship Proper

Student shall focus on internship aiming to gain industry experience and professional development.

Week 24: Final Completion and Graduation

Student shall finalize all thier works and recieve a certificate on Graduation day, for their hard work in the class session and their dedication during the internship.
