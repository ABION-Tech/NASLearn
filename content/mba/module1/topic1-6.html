Common Misconceptions

1. Misconception:

"A corporation is always the best choice for a business structure."

Clarification:

Not always. While corporations offer limited liability and easier access to capital, they come with higher regulatory and tax obligations. For small businesses, a sole proprietorship or LLC may be a better option.
