Practice Exercises

Scenario 1: A sole proprietor wants to expand their business and is considering incorporating. List the pros and cons of incorporation and recommend whether they should proceed, explaining your reasoning.
Solution:
  • Pros: Limited liability, easier to raise capital, perpetual existence.
  • Cons: Higher administrative costs, more regulatory requirements.
Scenario 2: A company wants to launch a new product but needs to ensure efficient operations and marketing. Develop a brief plan covering key aspects of operations and marketing strategies for the product launch.
Solution:
  • Operations: Streamline production, ensure supply chain readiness.
  • Marketing: Identify target audience, develop a promotion plan, set pricing.
