Discussion Questions

1. How do different types of business structures impact the risks and rewards for entrepreneurs?
Consider discussing how sole proprietorships may have unlimited liability, whereas corporations limit personal risk.
2. In what ways can businesses influence the overall economy? Are these influences always positive?
Explore how businesses can create jobs and drive innovation but also consider negative impacts like environmental damage.
3. How can a company’s choice of management style affect its culture and performance?
Discuss the differences between authoritarian, democratic, and laissez-faire management styles and their effects on team dynamics.
4. Consider a product you use daily. How do you think the company markets it to different consumer groups?
Analyze marketing strategies that target demographics based on age, income, or lifestyle, and how they affect purchasing decisions.
5. What role do financial decisions play in a business’s ability to innovate and grow?
Reflect on how investment in R&D and budgeting impacts a company's capacity to develop new products and enter new markets.
