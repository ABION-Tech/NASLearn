Practice Exercises

Exercise 1: Market Entry Strategy Analysis

Choose a local business that you think has the potential for international expansion. Research the industry and the target market, then recommend a suitable market entry strategy (e.g., exporting, franchising, joint ventures, or FDI). Justify your decision based on market dynamics, risks, and cultural considerations.

Solution Example:

  • Business: A local coffee shop in the U.S. looking to enter the European market.
  • Market Entry Strategy: Joint venture with a local café chain to leverage local knowledge and distribution networks.
  • Justification: Europe’s strong coffee culture, combined with established competitors, requires a partnership to navigate cultural preferences and regulations.

Exercise 2: Cultural Consideration Case Study

Analyze how a company successfully adapted to cultural differences when expanding globally. What were the specific cultural challenges, and how did the company address them?

Solution Example:

  • Company: Coca-Cola in China.
  • Cultural Challenge: Initially faced resistance to the taste of its product in China, where consumers preferred herbal and traditional beverages.
  • Solution: Introduced products like herbal teas and flavors that catered to local tastes while maintaining its global brand.
