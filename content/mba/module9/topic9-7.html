Assignment: Global Market Entry Strategy

Research and Present

Research and present a global market entry strategy for a local business of your choice. Your analysis should include:

  • Cultural Considerations: Examine the cultural aspects that the business must consider when entering a new market. Discuss how these factors can affect marketing strategies, product offerings, and customer relations.
  • Trade Agreements: Identify relevant trade agreements that could impact the business’s operations and market entry strategy. Explain how these agreements facilitate or hinder trade between countries.
  • Political/Economic Risks: Analyze the political and economic risks associated with the chosen market. Consider aspects such as political stability, exchange rates, and regulatory environments that may affect the business’s success.
