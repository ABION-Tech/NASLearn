Common Misconceptions

1. Misconception:

"Marketing is just about advertising."

Clarification: While promotion (advertising) is one part of the marketing mix, marketing also involves product development, pricing strategies, and distribution channels, all of which contribute to a successful business strategy.

2. Misconception:

"Market research is only about surveys."

Clarification: Market research includes a wide range of techniques, from qualitative focus groups to quantitative data analysis. Surveys are just one tool in a broader toolkit.
