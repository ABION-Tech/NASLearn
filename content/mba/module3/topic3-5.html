Practice Exercises

1. Scenario 1:

A new company wants to launch an eco-friendly smartphone accessory (e.g., a biodegradable phone case). Create a marketing mix (4 Ps) for this product.

Solution:

  • Product: A phone case made of biodegradable materials, available in various colors and designs.
  • Price: Mid-range pricing to reflect its premium, eco-friendly materials.
  • Place: Sold online and in eco-conscious retail stores.
  • Promotion: Targeted ads on social media platforms focusing on sustainability and environmental impact.

2. Scenario 2:

You are tasked with conducting market research for a fitness app targeting young professionals. Design a research plan using both qualitative and quantitative methods.

Solution:

  • Qualitative: Focus groups to explore motivations behind fitness habits and challenges faced by young professionals.
  • Quantitative: Online surveys to gather data on exercise frequency, preferred workout types, and willingness to pay for fitness apps.
