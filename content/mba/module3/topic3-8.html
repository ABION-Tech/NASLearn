  • Develop a marketing plan for a hypothetical product of your choice. Include an analysis of the marketing mix, a brief market research plan, and strategies for brand positioning.
  • Case study analysis: Analyze the brand strategy of a successful company like Apple or Nike, focusing on their positioning, brand identity, and consumer loyalty strategies.

Practical Applications

  1. Career Application:

    The marketing mix (4 Ps) is a core framework that professionals in marketing, sales, and product development use to create effective marketing strategies. For example, in product management roles, understanding pricing and promotion is critical when launching new products.

  2. Real-World Scenario:

    Consider a startup launching a new app. The marketing mix ensures that each aspect—product features, price point, distribution channels, and promotional tactics—is aligned to attract the right customer base. This framework can help the startup avoid costly missteps, like launching in the wrong market or at the wrong price.
