Discussion Questions

1. How can ethical decision-making frameworks help businesses resolve conflicts between profitability and social responsibility?
2. In what ways can corporate governance prevent ethical scandals and build stakeholder trust?
3. How can a company balance profit-making with its corporate social responsibility efforts? Is there a trade-off between profitability and social good?
4. What ethical responsibilities do businesses have to their employees, the environment, and society as a whole?
5. Should businesses voluntarily adopt higher ethical standards than those required by law? Why or why not?
