Practice Exercises

1. Exercise 1: Ethical Dilemma Scenario

Imagine you are a manager at a company facing a major ethical dilemma: you have information that one of your products has a defect that could potentially harm customers, but recalling it will cost the company millions. How would you approach this decision using one of the ethical frameworks discussed?

Solution Example:
  • Utilitarian Approach: You would recall the product, prioritizing customer safety over profit. The overall benefit (avoiding harm to customers) outweighs the financial cost.
2. Exercise 2: Developing a CSR Strategy

Select a company of your choice. Identify three key areas where the company can improve its CSR practices. Develop a brief strategy that addresses these areas and includes stakeholder engagement and performance metrics.

Solution Example (Starbucks):
  • Focus Areas: Environmental sustainability (reducing plastic waste), employee well-being (fair wages and healthcare), and ethical sourcing (sustainable coffee farming).
  • CSR Strategy: Starbucks could enhance its commitment to eliminating single-use plastics, improve healthcare benefits for all employees, and ensure its coffee beans are ethically sourced through fair trade practices.
