Navigating Ethical Dilemmas in Leadership

Business leaders often face difficult ethical choices. Understanding ethical frameworks and corporate governance will help future leaders make informed decisions that align with both the company's objectives and societal values.

- **Key Concepts:**

  • Ethical Decision-Making: Leaders must consider the implications of their choices on all stakeholders.
  • Corporate Governance: Ensuring that decisions adhere to established policies and ethical standards.
  • Transparency: Maintaining open communication with stakeholders to build trust and accountability.
