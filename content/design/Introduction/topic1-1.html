About This Course

Welcome to the Graphics Design Course at the New Age Skillsphere (NAS). This course is designed to equip you with the essential skills in graphic design, enabling you to create visually appealing and effective designs for both digital and print media. Whether you're new to design or looking to enhance your current skill set, this course will provide you with a solid foundation in the principles and techniques of graphic design.

Throughout the course, you will engage with expert-led lectures, hands-on projects, case studies, and collaborative activities. You will learn how to use industry-standard design tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign to create graphics, logos, layouts, and other visual elements. You'll also explore advanced design techniques, including typography, color theory, branding, and visual communication, which are essential for producing professional-quality work.

Our unique learning model ensures you balance theoretical knowledge with practical application during the class phase and in your internship.

Learning Model

  • Self-paced lessons available on the LMS
  • Weekly group discussion sessions
  • Hands-on projects and peer collaboration
  • Internship opportunities for real-world experience

Total Duration: 6 months

The entire program lasts 6 months and combines coursework, electives, and an internship for a comprehensive learning experience to equip you for the future.

Core Program

10 weeks of intensive coursework

We will provide you with practical digital skills through real-world case studies and lessons that directly apply to industry needs.

Electives

2 additional weeks

During these two weeks, you'll have the opportunity to select one elective per week, choosing from exciting from subjects like AI, Entrepreneurship, Digital Marketing, and more to broaden your skills.

Internship

3-month internal internship at ABION Industries

Gain hands-on experience working on real projects within ABION Industries, preparing you for your future career or entrepreneurial goals.

Graphics Design Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Graphic Design

Understand the basics of graphic design, its purpose in visual communication, and how it impacts various industries.

Module 2: Design Principles and Theory

Explore the fundamental design principles, including balance, contrast, alignment, and hierarchy, and how to apply them effectively.

Module 3: Typography in Graphic Design

Learn how to choose fonts, manage spacing, and create readable, aesthetically pleasing type settings that complement your designs.

Module 4: Color Theory and Application

Dive into color psychology and the effective use of color in design, including creating harmonious palettes and understanding contrasts.

Module 5: Digital Imaging and Software Tools

Gain hands-on experience with popular graphic design software like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to enhance your design skills.

Module 6: Branding and Identity Design

Learn the fundamentals of brand identity, including logo design, brand color schemes, and creating cohesive branding across various media.

Module 7: Print and Digital Layout Design

Master the art of layout design, creating effective compositions for both print media (e.g., brochures) and digital platforms (e.g., websites).

Module 8: User Experience (UX) in Design

Understand the basics of UX design, including user research, wireframing, and creating intuitive designs that meet user needs.

Module 9: Motion Graphics and Animation

Learn how to animate designs and create motion graphics using tools like Adobe After Effects to add dynamism to your work.

Week 10: Capstone Project Execution

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 11 - 12: Elective Classes

Student shall embark on a two-week journey on any 2 electives availble on our catalogue to broaden their horizons.

Week 13: Internship Preparation

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 14-23: Internship Proper

Student shall focus on internship aiming to gain industry experience and professional development.

Week 24: Final Completion and Graduation

Student shall finalize all thier works and recieve a certificate on Graduation day, for their hard work in the class session and their dedication during the internship.
