Objective

The goal of this project is to create a **fully edited, professional-level digital artwork** using Photoshop, Illustrator, or similar software. This project will integrate various digital imaging techniques learned in previous lessons.

Project Requirements

To complete this project, you must incorporate:

  • **High-quality image composition** (at least two combined images).
  • **Layer masking** for seamless blending.
  • **Retouching techniques** (removal of imperfections, enhancements).
  • **Color correction & grading** (Curves, Selective Color, or Gradient Maps).
  • **At least one special effect** (Double Exposure, Glitch, Motion Blur, etc.).
  • **Typography integration** (if applicable, to enhance the visual story).
  • **Smart Objects for flexibility** in design adjustments.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Planning Your Artwork

Before starting, consider the theme, mood, and visual style of your design.

  • Decide on the **subject** (portrait, landscape, abstract design, etc.).
  • Gather **high-resolution images** to use as base elements.
  • Sketch a **rough composition** (either digitally or on paper).

2. Setting Up the Canvas

  1. Open Photoshop (or preferred software) and create a **new document** (1920x1080 px recommended).
  2. Import all selected images as separate layers.
  3. Organize layers into groups for better workflow.

3. Image Blending & Layer Masking

  • Use **Layer Masks** to combine multiple images smoothly.
  • Apply **Soft Brushes** to refine edges for natural transitions.
  • Experiment with **Blending Modes** (Overlay, Multiply, etc.) for unique effects.

4. Retouching & Enhancements

  • Remove distractions with the **Healing Brush Tool**.
  • Use **Dodge & Burn** to add contrast and depth.
  • Adjust sharpness and clarity using the **High-Pass Filter**.

5. Color Grading & Special Effects

  • Apply **Curves or Selective Color** for color correction.
  • Add cinematic effects using **Gradient Maps**.
  • Incorporate **special effects** like Glitch, Double Exposure, or Neon Glow.

6. Adding Typography & Final Adjustments

  • If applicable, add a title, quote, or branding using the **Text Tool**.
  • Use **Smart Objects** for non-destructive editing.
  • Apply **final sharpening and contrast adjustments** before exporting.

Submission Guidelines

Once you have completed your artwork, submit it as follows:

  • Save the file as a **.PSD (Photoshop) or .AI (Illustrator)** for editing.
  • Export the final image as a **JPEG or PNG (1920x1080 resolution minimum).**
  • Upload your project to an online portfolio or share in the class forum.
