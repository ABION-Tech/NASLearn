Activities & Assessments

Weekly Quiz

Covering key concepts including:

  • Motion graphics vs. traditional animation distinctions
  • Keyframe animation terminology and techniques
  • Kinetic typography applications
  • Visual effects and compositing concepts
  • Tool selection criteria
Assignment

Develop a Complete Brand Identity Package

Create a motion graphics package that includes:

  • Animated logo reveal (3-5 seconds)
  • Animated typography system demonstrating kinetic principles
  • Lower third templates for video content
  • Social media animated elements (stories, posts)
  • Documentation of animation choices and brand alignment
Discussion Questions
  • How does motion enhance or change the message of static design elements?
  • What considerations are unique to designing for motion versus static media?
  • How can animation principles reinforce brand identity consistently?
  • When does motion become distracting rather than enhancing?
Hands-On Practice

Create Branded Graphics for Multiple Platforms

Develop animated graphics for:

  • Instagram Story that demonstrates brand personality
  • LinkedIn professional presentation opener
  • Website banner that captures attention without being distracting

Ensure consistency across platforms while adapting to different format requirements.

Submission Requirements
  • Source files for all motion graphics projects
  • Exported videos in appropriate formats
  • Design documentation explaining creative choices
  • Reflection on challenges and learning outcomes
