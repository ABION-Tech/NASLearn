Assignment: Creating an Audience Persona

Objective:

Develop a detailed and actionable audience persona for a specific product or service, showcasing how this understanding informs content strategies.

Steps:

1. Choose a Product or Service

Select a real or fictional product/service.

Examples:

  • A luxury skincare line targeting eco-conscious consumers.
  • A fictional meal prep service catering to busy urban professionals.
2. Research

Simulate or conduct research to gather insights about the target audience.

Suggested tools and methods:

  • Google Analytics: Analyze hypothetical website visitor data for demographics and behaviors.
  • Social Media Insights: Study trends, engagement metrics, and follower demographics for a similar product/service.
  • Surveys or Online Research:
    • Example survey question: "What factors influence your purchasing decisions for skincare products?"
    • Online research: Study market reports or customer reviews for similar products.
3. Create the Persona

Define key characteristics:

  • Demographics:
    • Age, gender, income, location, education, occupation.
  • Goals and Motivations:
    • What the audience wants to achieve (e.g., "Feel confident in their skin").
  • Challenges and Pain Points:
    • Barriers they face (e.g., "Difficulty finding products with clean ingredients").
  • Communication Preferences:
    • Preferred channels (e.g., Instagram for ads, email for discounts).

Visual Element: Include a stock photo, icon, or sketch to personify the audience.

Example: A young professional with a laptop and reusable coffee cup.

4. Submit

Written Persona Description (150–200 words):

Provide an overview combining demographics, goals, challenges, and preferences.

Explanation (100–150 words):

Discuss how the persona would influence content strategies.

Example: “This persona highlights the need for visually engaging Instagram content paired with informative blog posts focusing on eco-friendly benefits.”

Evaluation Criteria
  • Depth and Clarity: Persona should offer a well-rounded view of the audience, reflecting thoughtful analysis.
  • Creativity and Practicality: The persona should feel relatable and actionable for guiding content strategies.
  • Supporting Research and Rationale: Data, even simulated, should support the persona’s details and its impact on content decisions.

Conclusion:

This assignment not only builds research and analytical skills but also equips students with a practical tool for content development success.
