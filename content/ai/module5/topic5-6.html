Week 5: Chatbots and Conversational AI

Discussion Questions & Assignments

Discussion Questions

Engage with these thought-provoking questions to deepen your understanding of chatbots and conversational AI.

  1. What are the key differences between rule-based chatbots and AI-powered chatbots? Provide real-world examples of each.
  2. In what ways can conversational AI improve customer experience in various industries?
  3. What are the potential ethical concerns surrounding AI chatbots? How can businesses address these concerns?
  4. How does Natural Language Processing (NLP) enhance chatbot interactions? Provide an example of a chatbot using NLP effectively.
  5. Should chatbots aim to be indistinguishable from humans, or should they always disclose their AI nature? Why?
  6. What are some major challenges in developing and deploying an AI chatbot? How can these challenges be mitigated?

Assignment

Apply your knowledge by completing the following tasks.

Task 1: Chatbot Analysis

Find and interact with at least two different chatbots (e.g., customer service bots, virtual assistants, or AI-powered chatbots).

  • Document their responses to different queries.
  • Analyze their strengths and weaknesses.
  • Compare how well they handle user input, understand context, and provide useful responses.

Task 2: Design a Chatbot Flowchart

Using a flowchart tool (e.g., Figma, Lucidchart, Miro), design a conversation flow for a chatbot that assists users in a specific scenario, such as:

  • Booking an appointment.
  • Providing product recommendations.
  • Answering frequently asked questions.

Ensure your flowchart includes:

  • Greeting and introduction.
  • Decision points based on user input.
  • Fallback responses for misunderstood queries.
  • An option to escalate to a human agent.

Task 3: Prototype a Simple Chatbot

Using a no-code chatbot builder (e.g., Chatfuel, ManyChat, Landbot), create a simple chatbot prototype that:

  • Welcomes users.
  • Answers at least three different questions.
  • Includes a feedback mechanism (e.g., "Did I answer your question? Yes/No").

Submit a link or screenshots of your chatbot in action.

Task 4: Research & Report

Write a short report (500-700 words) on the future of conversational AI, covering:

  • Recent advancements in chatbot technology.
  • Potential future applications in business and daily life.
  • Challenges and ethical concerns that might arise.
