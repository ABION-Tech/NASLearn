Assignment

Prompt Engineering

Task 1: Design three distinct prompts for a conversational AI model addressing different use cases:

  • Customer service (e.g., “How can I reset my password?”)
  • Education (e.g., “Explain the principle of photosynthesis in simple terms.”)
  • Creative writing (e.g., “Write a short poem about the sunset.”)

Task 2: Test and refine the prompts using variations to achieve the desired outcomes. Share your process, and explain the adjustments made to improve the responses.
