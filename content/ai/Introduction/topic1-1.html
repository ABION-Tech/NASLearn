About This Course

This course is designed to give you a comprehensive understanding of AI. Over the next several weeks, you will engage with expert-led lectures, hands-on projects, and collaborative learning activities.

Our unique learning model ensures you balance theoretical knowledge with practical application during the class phase and in your internship.

Learning Model

  • Self-paced lessons available on the LMS
  • Weekly group discussion sessions
  • Hands-on projects and peer collaboration
  • Internship opportunities for real-world experience

Total Duration: 6 months

The entire program lasts 6 months and combines coursework, electives, and an internship for a comprehensive learning experience to equip you for the future.

Core Program

10 weeks of intensive coursework

We will provide you with practical digital skills through real-world case studies and lessons that directly apply to industry needs.

Electives

2 additional weeks

During these two weeks, you'll have the opportunity to select one elective per week, choosing from exciting from subjects like Web Development, Entrepreneurship, Digital Marketing, and more to broaden your skills.

Internship

3-month internal internship at ABION Industries

Gain hands-on experience working on real projects within ABION Industries, preparing you for your future career or entrepreneurial goals.

Course Curriculum

Week 1: Introduction to AI and Its Business Applications

Get familiar with the basics of Artificial Intelligence, its relevance in business, potential benefits of leveraging AI technologies and its applications in the industry.

Week 2: Text Generation and Its Business Applications

Explore text generation technologies and their practical applications in content creation and business communication.

Week 3: Prompt Engineering Basics-Advance

Learn from fundamentals of prompt engineering to advanced techniques and its importance in refining and optimizing AI tool performance.

Week 4: AI for Image, Audio & Video Generation

Explore the potential of AI for creating images, generating and enhancing audio and transforming visual content production.

Week 5: Chatbots and Conversational AI

Explores how AI is used to power chatbots and conversational agents, improving customer service and user engagement.

Week 6: AI in Coding: Software & Web Development

Focus on how AI is transforming the world of software development, offering tools that help automate coding and debugging.

Week 7: Integrating AI into Business Workflows

Learn how to seamlessly integrate AI tools into existing business processes for operational improvement and innovation.

Week 8: Data Management and Ethics in AI

Address data management practices and ethical considerations when using AI in business.

Week 9: Future Trends in AI

Explore emerging trends and future developments in AI, focusing on their potential impact on business.

Week 10: Capstone Project Execution

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 11 - 12: Elective Classes

Student shall embark on a two-week journey on any 2 electives availble on our catalogue to broaden their horizons.

Week 13: Internship Preparation

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 14-23: Internship Proper

Student shall focus on internship aiming to gain industry experience and professional development.

Week 24: Final Completion and Graduation

Student shall finalize all thier works and recieve a certificate on Graduation day, for their hard work in the class session and their dedication during the internship.

The Class Phase

In this phase, you will have access to all course materials via the LMS, complete the lessons,assignments, quizes and participate in the weekly discussions. This activities will support your learning.

The Internship

After the class phase, transition to a 3-month internship to apply your knowledge in real-world projects within Our Start-up ABION Industries, giving you practical, career-ready experience.
