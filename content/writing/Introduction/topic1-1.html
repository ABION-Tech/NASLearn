About This Course

Welcome to the Writing Course at the New Age Skillsphere (NAS). This comprehensive course is designed to empower you with the skills needed to excel in various forms of writing, including creative, technical, and copywriting. Whether you're a budding writer or a seasoned professional seeking to refine your craft, this course will equip you with the tools to communicate ideas effectively and create compelling content for diverse audiences.

Throughout the course, you will engage with expert-led lectures, interactive workshops, real-world writing projects, and peer collaborations. You will learn essential writing techniques, including narrative development, persuasive writing, research-based content creation, and editing for clarity and impact. Additionally, you'll explore the nuances of storytelling, brand voice, SEO-driven copywriting, and technical documentation.

Our unique learning approach ensures a balance between theoretical knowledge and practical application. You will develop a versatile writing portfolio, master the art of crafting impactful messages, and gain confidence in delivering content tailored to various industries and git status platforms. The course also includes an internship, offering hands-on experience in professional writing scenarios, preparing you for a successful career in writing across multiple disciplines.

Learning Model

  • Self-paced lessons available on the LMS
  • Weekly group discussion sessions

  • Internship opportunities for real-world experience

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Writing

An introduction to the world of writing, covering the different writing styles, purposes, and the essential skills for aspiring writers.

Module 2: Grammar and Sentence Structure

Focus on the core components of grammar and sentence construction, helping you create clear, precise, and effective sentences.

Module 3: Writing for Different Mediums

Learn how to write for various mediums such as blogs, websites, social media, print media, and more, while maintaining the unique tone and style for each.

Module 4: Creative Writing Techniques

Explore creative writing techniques, including storytelling, character development, dialogue, and narrative voice, to make your writing come alive.

Module 5: Writing for Business

Master writing in a professional context, including business emails, reports, proposals, and presentations, with an emphasis on clarity and conciseness.

Module 6: Editing and Proofreading

Learn essential editing and proofreading techniques to improve your writing, catch errors, and enhance overall readability.

Module 7: Writing Persuasively

Develop persuasive writing skills for crafting compelling arguments, including writing sales copy, advertisements, and persuasive articles.

Module 8: Writing for SEO

Learn how to write for search engines, focusing on keyword optimization, on-page SEO, and the best practices for creating content that ranks.

Module 9: Building Your Writing Portfolio

Create a professional writing portfolio showcasing your best work, including selecting the right projects and presenting them effectively to potential clients.

Week 10: Capstone Project Execution

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 11 - 12: Elective Classes

Student shall embark on a two-week journey on any 2 electives availble on our catalogue to broaden their horizons.

Week 13: Internship Preparation

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 14-23: Internship Proper

Student shall focus on internship aiming to gain industry experience and professional development.

Week 24: Final Completion and Graduation

Student shall finalize all thier works and recieve a certificate on Graduation day, for their hard work in the class session and their dedication during the internship.
