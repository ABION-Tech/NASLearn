Assignment: Edit a 1-Minute Video

Objective:

Create a polished 1-minute video using any video editing software covered in this module. The video should showcase your editing skills and creativity. Focus on smooth transitions, effective titles, and basic color grading to enhance the visual appeal of the final product.

Instructions:
  • Source Material: Use pre-recorded clips (e.g., stock footage, personal recordings) or shoot your own. Ensure the clips fit together cohesively to tell a story within the 1-minute timeframe.
  • Editing Requirements:
    • Transitions: Include at least two distinct transitions, such as fades, dissolves, or other creative options. Choose transitions that flow well with your video’s pacing and narrative.
    • Titles:
      • Opening Title: Add a title at the beginning of the video to introduce it. The title should be clear, readable, and aligned with the theme of the video.
      • Closing Credits: Include closing credits with your name or acknowledgment of any resources used, such as stock footage, music, or sound effects. Make sure it’s professional and visually appealing.
    • Color Grading: Apply basic color grading adjustments such as brightness, contrast, and saturation to enhance the visual quality of the video. Ensure that the video’s colors are consistent and complement the mood you want to convey.
  • Export: Render the final video at 720p resolution or higher. Ensure that the video file size is manageable for submission, and check that the video is clear and sharp.
Submission Guidelines:
  1. Export the final video file in a commonly supported format (e.g., MP4, MOV, AVI).
  2. Write a brief description (150–200 words) detailing:
    • Editing choices made, such as why you selected particular transitions and effects.
    • Reasons for selecting specific transitions and effects to enhance the video.
    • Challenges faced during editing and how you addressed them to achieve a polished result.
Evaluation Criteria:
  • Creativity and Coherence: Smooth and relevant transitions that enhance the storytelling of the video. Ensure the editing flows naturally and aligns with the overall theme.
  • Titles and Color Grading: Clear and professional titles, along with effective visual enhancement through color grading, that complement the video's style and tone.
  • Technical Accuracy: The video should be edited and exported properly with a resolution of 720p or higher. The final file should be smooth, with no visible glitches or errors.
