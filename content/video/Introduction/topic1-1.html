About This Course

Welcome to the Video Editing Course at the New Age Skillsphere (NAS). This course is designed to equip you with the essential skills in video editing, enabling you to create compelling, professional-quality videos that captivate audiences. Whether you're new to video editing or looking to enhance your existing skills, this course will provide you with a solid foundation in the techniques, tools, and creative processes involved in video production.

Throughout the course, you will engage with expert-led lectures, hands-on projects, case studies, and collaborative activities. You will learn how to use industry-standard video editing software such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and DaVinci Resolve to cut, edit, and enhance video content. You'll explore key concepts like color grading, sound design, visual effects, and motion graphics to add polish and creativity to your projects. Additionally, you'll gain insight into storytelling and pacing, which are crucial for making your videos impactful and engaging.

Our unique learning model ensures you gain both technical proficiency and creative skills in video editing. You will develop the ability to produce high-quality video content from start to finish, mastering every step of the process from footage collection to final edits. The course also includes an internship, where you'll work on real-world video editing projects, allowing you to apply your skills in a professional setting and preparing you for success in the growing field of video production.

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Video Editing

Learn the basics of video editing, the software used, and the fundamental concepts behind editing video content.

Module 2: Video Editing Software

Get hands-on experience with popular video editing software, including Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve.

Module 3: Basic Editing Techniques

Learn how to cut, trim, and splice video clips to create a cohesive story, along with basic transitions and effects.

Module 4: Audio Editing and Sound Design

Understand the importance of audio in video editing and learn how to edit, sync, and enhance audio tracks effectively.

Module 5: Color Correction and Grading

Learn how to adjust color balance, contrast, and brightness in video clips to achieve professional-quality footage.

Module 6: Advanced Editing Techniques

Explore advanced editing techniques, such as keyframing, motion graphics, multi-camera editing, and advanced transitions.

Module 7: Special Effects and Compositing

Learn how to integrate special effects and composites, including green screen techniques and motion tracking.

Module 8: Exporting and File Management

Understand the different file formats for video export, how to optimize video for different platforms, and effective file management practices.

Module 9: Video Production Workflow

Master the full video production workflow, from pre-production to editing and final delivery, ensuring efficiency and quality in every project.

Week 10: Capstone Project Execution

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 11 - 12: Elective Classes

Student shall embark on a two-week journey on any 2 electives availble on our catalogue to broaden their horizons.

Week 13: Internship Preparation

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 14-23: Internship Proper

Student shall focus on internship aiming to gain industry experience and professional development.

Week 24: Final Completion and Graduation

Student shall finalize all thier works and recieve a certificate on Graduation day, for their hard work in the class session and their dedication during the internship.
