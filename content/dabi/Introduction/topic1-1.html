About This Course

Welcome to the Data Analysis and Business Intelligence Course at the New Age Skillsphere (NAS). This course is designed to equip you with the essential skills in data analysis, enabling you to interpret data and transform it into actionable insights that can drive business decisions. Whether you're new to the field or looking to enhance your current skill set, this course will provide you with a solid foundation in data analysis and business intelligence techniques.

Throughout the course, you will engage with expert-led lectures, hands-on projects, case studies, and collaborative activities. You will learn how to work with data using powerful tools, such as Excel, SQL, and BI platforms, to clean, visualize, and analyze data. You'll also explore advanced techniques like predictive modeling, statistical analysis, and reporting that are essential for uncovering patterns and trends in complex datasets.

Our unique learning model ensures you balance theoretical knowledge with practical application during the class phase and in your internship.

Learning Model

  • Self-paced lessons available on the LMS
  • Weekly group discussion sessions
  • Hands-on projects and peer collaboration
  • Internship opportunities for real-world experience

Data Analysis and Business Intelligence Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Data Analysis

Learn the fundamentals of data analysis, including data types, basic statistics, and the role of a data analyst in organizations.

Module 2: Excel for Data Analysis

Master Excel tools and functions such as pivot tables, VLOOKUP, and complex formulas used for analyzing and visualizing data.

Module 3: SQL and Database Management

Get hands-on experience with SQL queries to extract, manipulate, and analyze data from databases effectively.

Module 4: Data Visualization Tools (Power BI, Tableau)

Learn how to visualize data through interactive dashboards and reports using tools like Power BI and Tableau.

Module 5: Statistical Analysis and Predictive Modeling

Understand statistical methods for data analysis, and learn how to build predictive models to forecast trends and outcomes.

Module 6: Business Intelligence Strategy and Reporting

Learn how to use business intelligence to drive decision-making and communicate insights effectively through data-driven reports.

Module 7: Data Ethics and Security

Understand the ethical implications of data collection and analysis, as well as the importance of data security and privacy protection.

Module 8: Data Cleaning and Preparation

Learn the techniques for cleaning, transforming, and preparing raw data to make it usable for analysis and reporting.

Module 9: Advanced Business Intelligence Techniques

Explore advanced BI tools and techniques like machine learning integration and advanced reporting strategies.

Week 10: Capstone Project Execution

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 11 - 12: Elective Classes

Student shall embark on a two-week journey on any 2 electives availble on our catalogue to broaden their horizons.

Week 13: Internship Preparation

Prepare for your internship by focusing on industry expectations and professional development.

Week 14-23: Internship Proper

Student shall focus on internship aiming to gain industry experience and professional development.

Week 24: Final Completion and Graduation

Student shall finalize all thier works and recieve a certificate on Graduation day, for their hard work in the class session and their dedication during the internship.
