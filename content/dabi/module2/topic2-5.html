Assignment: Sales Data Analysis and Dashboard Creation

Objective

Use Microsoft Excel to analyze a sales dataset and design a dynamic dashboard that visualizes key business insights.

Steps to Complete the Assignment

1. Dataset Preparation

Acquire Data:

  • Use a provided dataset or download a simple sales dataset online.
  • Ensure the dataset includes the following fields:
    • Date: Transaction date.
    • Region: Location of the sale.
    • Product Category: Group or type of product sold.
    • Sales Amount: Revenue generated per transaction.

2. Data Analysis

Create Pivot Tables:

  • Summarize sales data by:
    • Region: Total sales per geographic location.
    • Product Category: Revenue by product type.
    • Time Period: Monthly or quarterly sales trends.
  • Include calculations such as total sales, average sales, and percentage contribution to overall revenue.

3. Dashboard Creation

Design Layout:

  • Use a clean, professional layout that includes:
    • A title and clear section labels.
    • Organized visual elements for ease of understanding.

Interactive Slicers:

  • Add slicers to allow filtering by:
    • Product category.
    • Time period (e.g., months or quarters).

Charts for Visualization:

  • Include at least three types of charts to showcase:
    • Sales Trends: Line chart for monthly or quarterly sales performance.
    • Regional Comparison: Bar or column chart for sales by region.
    • Product Breakdown: Pie chart for percentage contribution by product category.

4. Submission Requirements

  • Submit a single Excel file that includes:
    • Pivot Tables: All tables used for summarizing data.
    • Dynamic Dashboard: The completed dashboard with slicers and charts.
    • Written Insights: A brief explanation (150–200 words) addressing the following:
      • Key trends observed in the sales data.
      • Insights into which regions or products performed best.
      • Suggestions for improving sales or focusing on high-performing areas.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Data Analysis: Accuracy and relevance of the pivot tables.
  • Dashboard Design: Professional, clear layout with intuitive slicers and well-labeled charts.
  • Insights and Explanation: Depth of insights derived from the data and the relevance to potential business decisions.

Assignment Overview

This assignment emphasizes hands-on experience with Excel’s data analysis and visualization features while reinforcing skills in deriving actionable business insights.
