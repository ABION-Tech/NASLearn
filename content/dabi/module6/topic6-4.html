Power BI Best Practices

Designing Effective Dashboards

Creating an effective Power BI dashboard requires careful consideration of layout, storytelling, and performance.

  • Layout and Structure: Organizing visualizations for clarity and flow in Power BI dashboards.
  • Data Storytelling: Using Power BI to tell a compelling data-driven story and convey insights effectively.
  • Performance Optimization: Improving dashboard performance by optimizing data models and queries.

Collaboration and Sharing

Sharing insights and collaborating with stakeholders is a crucial part of the Power BI workflow.

  • Publishing and Sharing: Publishing Power BI reports to Power BI Service and sharing them with stakeholders. Using Power BI Apps for controlled distribution.

Practical Exercise

Hands-On Practice: Design and develop a comprehensive dashboard in Power BI. Incorporate best practices for layout, storytelling, and performance optimization. Publish the dashboard to Power BI Service and share it with peers for feedback.
