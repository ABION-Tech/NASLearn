Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (Machine Learning)

Overview

Welcome to the world of Machine Learning a subset of AI, which focuses on training algorithms on datasets to identify patterns and make decisions. Machine learning is at the core of most AI applications, hence its rise in demand. It's the process of teaching a computer to make predictions or decisions based on data. This course is designed to equip you with the essential Machine Learning skills needed by many tech companies.

Throughout the course, we’ll explore everything from Supervised learning, Unsupervised learning, Deep learning, Neural Networks and more. By the end, you'll successfully gain a solid foundation in machine learning and be ready to apply the knowledge in a practical setting.

The course is structured in a hands-on manner, allowing you to apply what you learn immediately through coding exercises and real-world projects. Our final goal is not only to teach you theory but to help you build a solid portfolio that demonstrates your skills to potential employers or clients.

Ethical Considerations & Social Impact

AI presents both exciting opportunities and complex challenges. Ethical considerations include:

  • Bias in AI: AI systems can inherit biases from the data they are trained on, leading to unfair outcomes.
  • Privacy: AI-driven surveillance and data collection can infringe on individual privacy rights.
  • Job Displacement: Automation could replace many jobs, necessitating a focus on upskilling and new employment opportunities.

In this course, we'll cover both front-end and back-end technologies, helping you become a full-stack developer by the time you complete it. We’ll take a step-by-step approach, starting with the basics before diving into more complex topics.

By the end of this course, you’ll understand how all these pieces work together to build robust web applications, and you’ll have the confidence to tackle projects of your own. Whether your goal is to become a freelance developer, work for a tech company, or simply build your own personal projects, this course will set you on the right path.
