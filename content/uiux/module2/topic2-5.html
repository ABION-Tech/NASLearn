Assignment: Persona Creation

Objective:

Conduct a mock user interview and create a detailed user persona based on the insights gathered.

Instructions:

1. Interview:
  • Identify a target user: Choose a person who fits the intended audience for your design.
  • Ask questions: Focus on their goals, challenges, behaviors, and preferences. Example questions include:
    • What are your main goals when using [a specific type of product/service]?
    • What challenges do you face with current options?
    • What features or aspects would improve your experience?
2. Create the Persona:
  • Compile the data: Synthesize the interview findings into a persona template.
  • Include details:
    • Name: Provide a fictional but relatable name.
    • Photo: Use an image that represents the persona (stock photo or illustration).
    • Demographics: Age, location, occupation, etc.
    • Goals: Primary objectives related to the product or service.
    • Challenges: Key pain points or obstacles faced by the user.
    • Behaviors and preferences: Relevant habits and expectations.

Deliverable:

The persona should be submitted in one of the following formats:

  • A written document: A detailed write-up in Word or PDF format.
  • A presentation: A visually engaging slide deck.

Example Template:

Persona Detail Example
Name Sarah Johnson
Age 34
Location New York
Occupation Marketing Manager
Goals Simplify shopping for eco-friendly products.
Challenges Difficulty navigating e-commerce platforms.
Behaviors and Preferences Prefers mobile-friendly designs, values sustainability, seeks curated recommendations.

Evaluation Criteria:

  • Quality of the interview questions: Were they relevant and insightful?
  • Clarity and depth of the persona: Does the persona effectively represent a target user?
  • Relevance of insights to design solutions: Are the goals and challenges tied to actionable design ideas?
