5. Assessment

Quiz

Quiz Questions:

  1. Define UI and UX design and explain their relationship:

    Consider the definitions of User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design. Discuss how UI focuses on the layout, visual elements, and interactive components, while UX encompasses the overall journey and satisfaction of the user.

  2. Describe the steps in the user-centered design process:

    Outline the phases involved in user-centered design, including research, design, prototyping, testing, and iteration. Emphasize the importance of understanding user needs at each stage.

  3. Explain the importance of good UX for product success:

    Discuss how good UX leads to higher user satisfaction, retention, and engagement, ultimately contributing to a product's success in the market.

Assignment

Project Overview:

Conduct a UX/UI analysis of a website or mobile app of your choice. Your analysis should include:

  1. Selection of Product:

    Choose a website or mobile app that you frequently use. Consider its relevance to your interests or its impact on users.

  2. User Experience Evaluation:

    Assess the overall user experience. Identify aspects such as navigation, accessibility, content organization, and user satisfaction.

  3. User Interface Evaluation:

    Analyze the design elements, including layout, typography, colors, and imagery. Consider how these elements affect usability and aesthetic appeal.

  4. Identify Areas for Improvement:

    Based on your evaluation, pinpoint specific areas where the UX and UI could be enhanced. Provide clear reasoning for each suggestion.

  5. Propose a Redesign:

    Utilize the principles learned in class to create a redesign proposal. Include sketches or wireframes to illustrate your ideas. Justify your design choices based on user-centered design principles.
