1: Project Time-Cost Analysis

Task: Select a real or hypothetical project and create a time-cost trade-off analysis. Identify which tasks are on the critical path and propose strategies to optimize both time and cost.

Guidelines:

  • Define project objectives, scope, and constraints.
  • Use a simple Gantt chart or timeline to map tasks.
  • Identify cost implications for accelerating or delaying tasks.
  • Submit a brief report with your analysis and recommendations.

2: Budgeting and Risk Assessment

Task: Develop a project budget that includes a risk contingency plan. Outline how you would allocate resources to manage time and cost uncertainties.

Guidelines:

  • Create an estimated budget breakdown for major project components.
  • Identify potential risks that could impact costs and timelines.
  • Define buffer amounts and justify why they are necessary.
  • Submit a detailed budget plan with risk mitigation strategies.
