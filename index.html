About New Age Skillsphere

Empowering Africa's Youth with Future‑Proof Skills

New Age Skillsphere (NAS) is the flagship education initiative of ABION Industries. Born from our commitment to innovation and African empowerment, NAS bridges the continent's digital skills gap by offering zero‑cost, top‑tier training in high‑demand fields.

Our Mission

NAS exists to reshape Africa's talent pipeline. By fusing ABION's innovation‑driven culture with a proprietary, step‑by‑step mastery system, we transform motivated learners into confident, work‑ready professionals. Graduates earn a three‑month paid internship at ABION Industries—so you don't just learn, you do.
