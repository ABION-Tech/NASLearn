ABION Founders

Building Africa's Future

ABION Industries was founded in 2025 by visionary entrepreneur Ayomide Toluwase Joshua and the ABION Brothers with a singular mission: to revolutionize Africa's growth trajectory and reshape global perceptions through innovation, excellence, and sustainability.

What began as a dream in Abuja has grown into a pan-African conglomerate operating across 21 sectors, from space technology to education. Our approach combines Afrocentric values with global ambitions, creating solutions that address Africa's unique challenges while competing on the world stage.

We believe in the power of African ingenuity to solve not just local problems, but global challenges. Our motto, "There's more to life than we see or know," drives us to push beyond conventional boundaries and explore new frontiers of possibility.
