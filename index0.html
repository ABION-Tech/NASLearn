Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NAS

Is NAS really free?

Yes, 100% free. All course materials, mentorship, and the ABION internship opportunity are fully sponsored by ABION Industries as part of their commitment to developing African talent.

Who is eligible to apply?

We welcome applicants from across Africa with a passion for learning and growth. No prior technical background is required—we value motivation, aptitude, and potential over previous experience.

How does the ABION internship work?

Upon successful course completion, every graduate receives a three-month paid internship at ABION Industries. You'll work on real projects, build your professional portfolio, and establish valuable industry connections.

Do I need prior experience to join?

Not at all. Our proprietary NAS Technique is designed to accommodate learners at all levels, from complete beginners to those with some background. We meet you where you are and help you build expertise step by step.

What happens after I complete the program?

Beyond your ABION internship, you'll join our lifelong alumni community, offering ongoing career support, networking opportunities, and access to job openings across our partner network. Many graduates receive full-time employment offers directly from their internship experience.
